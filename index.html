The problem

IT shouldn't mean managing ten relationships.

You run an SME. You need software that works, systems you can trust, and advice you can act on — without hiring a CIO or signing ten contracts. Go supplier by supplier and you carry all of it: finding them, vetting them, chasing them when something breaks.

That's the part we take off your desk. We think every business deserves good technology without a big-company budget — so we built the pot instead of another agency. You get one relationship; behind it sits a bench of specialists we already know and trust.
